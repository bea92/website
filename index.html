Beatrice Gobbo

Drop me a line

I'm an academic at Warwick University, currently doing stuff as researcher and assistant professor at @Cimethods.

I received my Ph.D. in Design from the Design Department of Politecnico di Milano where I was a member of the DensityDesign Lab. My work and academic interests are positioned at the intersection between information design and computer science. In my PhD thesis “Embalming and Dissecting AI. Visual Explanations for the General Public” I describe a mixed methodology for approaching the Explainable Artificial Intelligence issue from a communication design perspective.
